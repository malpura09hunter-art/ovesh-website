Who Am I?

I'm Ovesh, a passionate cybersecurity enthusiast based in Mumbai. I believe that in today's hyperconnected world, digital safety is a fundamental right — not a luxury.

My journey into cybersecurity began with a curiosity about how systems work and, more importantly, how they can be protected. I focus on security awareness, OSINT research, and ethical learning — understanding vulnerabilities so defenders can be stronger.

I believe the first line of defense is an informed user. That's why I build tools and write content that educates people about staying safe online — from strong password practices to identifying phishing attempts.
